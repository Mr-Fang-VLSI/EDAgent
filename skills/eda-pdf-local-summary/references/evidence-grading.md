# Evidence Grading

Use this scale for each key claim in the summary:

1. `strong`
- backed by quantitative results or ablation from the paper.

2. `medium`
- supported by method description or limited empirical evidence.

3. `weak`
- inferred from discussion/background without direct measurement.

Rules:
1. Mark grade per claim, not per paper.
2. Avoid promoting weak claims into optimization decisions without additional validation.
