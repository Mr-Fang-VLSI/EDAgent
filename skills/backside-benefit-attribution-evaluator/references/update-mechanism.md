# Update Mechanism

Refresh this skill's summarized knowledge when:

## Refresh triggers

1. Gate D accumulates repeated three-arm evidence across more cases
2. the physical contract or PDN sufficiency contract changes
3. a repeated confounder is discovered in current comparison practice
4. new paper summaries materially change the expected attribution story

## What to update

1. refresh `background-knowledge-links.md`
2. update Gate D assumptions in `docs/knowledge_base/107_BSPDN_TOPOLOGY_AND_BENEFIT_VALIDATION_PROGRAM_20260307.md`
3. promote repeated attribution lessons into experiment experience artifacts through `eda-experiment-phenomenology-analyst`

## Promotion rule

Only revise the attribution narrative when repeated evidence changes the expected mechanism story.
Do not rewrite the narrative from one unstable or weakly evidenced compare pair.
