# Academic Slide Refiner Page-specific Practices

## Motivation Pages

- Clearly show the problem.
- Use contrasting visuals to highlight the performance gap.
- State why existing solutions are insufficient.

## "Why X In Y?" Pages

- Focus on the synergy, not on isolated component descriptions.
- Explain why the combination creates the advantage.

## Limitations Pages

- Be direct.
- Use simple language.
- A text-only centered layout with large fonts can be effective.
