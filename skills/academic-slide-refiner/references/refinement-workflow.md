# Academic Slide Refiner Workflow

Re-organize the presentation to follow a clear narrative:

`Problem -> Challenges -> Key Mechanism -> Why It Works -> Experimental Validation -> Conclusion`

Ensure there are clear transitions between these stages.

## Restructure And Split

- Re-order slides to fit the causal chain above.
- Split dense slides into multiple simpler slides.

Examples:
- VQ-VAE explanation:
  `1. What is VAE? -> 2. What is VQ-VAE? -> 3. Why VQ-VAE Helps Our Problem?`
- Bayesian Optimization explanation:
  `1. Why BO is Needed -> 2. How BO Works In Our Context`

## Content And Layout Refinement

- Ensure font size is large enough for room visibility (`>= 22pt` as a rule of thumb).
- Vertically center common left-text/right-image layouts.
- Keep images large and clear, roughly 40 to 60 percent of slide area.
- Keep diagrams stylistically consistent.
- Verify image paths to avoid broken images.
- Give all figures clear numbered captions.

## Academic Rigor

- Define acronyms on first appearance.
- Add citations for prior work and corresponding slide footnotes.
- Re-number references by slide appearance order, not by the original paper order.
